The Atlassian Connector for Eclipse is an Eclipse plugin that lets you work with the Atlassian products within your IDE. Now you don't have to switch between websites, email messages and news feeds to see what's happening to your project and your code. Instead, you can see the relevant JIRA issues and Bamboo build information right there in your development environment. Viewing your code in FishEye is just a click away.
